Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Here's a delicious recipe for chocolate chip cookies:

  1. Ingredients:
    • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tsp baking soda
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
    • 3/4 cup white sugar
    • 3/4 cup brown sugar
    • 2 eggs
    • 2 tsp vanilla extract
    • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  2. Instructions:
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. In a bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
    3. In a separate bowl, beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar together until light and fluffy.
    4. Add eggs and vanilla to butter mixture and beat until smooth.
    5. Gradually add in the flour mixture and mix until just combined.
    6. Stir in chocolate chips.
    7. Drop rounded spoonfuls of dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
    8. Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until golden brown.
    9. Cool on wire rack and enjoy!
